I'm No Longer Using Twitter for Professional Purposes
Written by Slade Rice Watkins • Published on Apr 12th, 2023

Today, I’m making the decision to no longer use Twitter for professional purposes. This means I will not advertise projects or other works on my personal Twitter handle going forward.

I will continue to use it, however, to connect with friends and mutual follows until the site inevitably dies. However, my posts will be unprofessional at best and I will basically only post memes and random stuff I come up with. It goes without saying – but as a reminder – my posts on any social media platform are my own thoughts and opinions, they do NOT represent any company I am affiliated with.

I can be found in many other places, as well as on Mastodon.

Thank you for your support! Let’s ride that bird site out into the sunset…

– Slade
